Evapayments

Evapayments is a React Application built for a client. Our team consisted of four members, each with specific roles. I served as the Frontend Developer, utilizing React to create an engaging user interface. We were also supported by a skilled Backend Developer specializing in PHP, a talented Graphics Designer, and a dedicated Content Creator. Together, we combined our expertise to build and deliver Evapayments, ensuring a comprehensive and well-rounded solution for our client.

Project Purpose and Goal

Evapayment is a cryptocurrency payment platform designed to empower both small and large businesses in broadening their reach beyond geographical limitations. By eliminating border barriers, Evapayment enables businesses to seamlessly connect with clients worldwide, fostering global accessibility. Its primary goal is to facilitate the expansion of businesses by providing a platform that transcends borders and connects them with potential customers across the globe.
